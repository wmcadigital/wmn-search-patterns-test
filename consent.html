Participation Information

WMCA is responsible for any information that you provide us. We ensure that this information will be processed in accordance with data protection legislation. We are committed to protecting your privacy and will use your personal information fairly.


Handling your data

The personal data that we will collect about you as part of this survey are:

  • Age
  • Email

We will safely store your personal data for a maximum of 12 months. This is so that the information you provide during this survey may be reused for further improvements to our transport services.

For further information on how we handle your information and your information rights, visit our Privacy Policy.


Your consent

By choosing to continue with these tasks, you are giving your informed consent to take part in our research.

You understand that you may remove yourself from the survey by closing the window before submitting your feedback, or requesting withdrawal at any time. If you choose to withdraw, we will delete your personal data from our research. However, your anonymous feedback to these tasks will be used to improve our services.
