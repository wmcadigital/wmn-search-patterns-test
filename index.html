Test our new campaign pages

We have designed a few different options for campaign pages and would love to hear your feedback on them.


The tasks

  • You will be asked to read the content on each page and locate specific information.
  • To complete a task, click on the part of the page when you find the required information.
  • Once you complete each task, you will need to answer some questions about the previous page.

Before you start

  • All links on the prototype pages will take you to the next page.
  • The pages are still being developed and may behave differently to what you might expect from a live website.
  • In the background, the prototype will anonymously record how you complete each task.
  • We use this information to see if our proposed designs work.
  • If you skip any of the questions you will not be entered in to the prize draw.
  • If you have an ad blocker enabled, you will need to turn it off, otherwise the questions will not appear.
  • The entire task will take 10 to 15 minutes.

A message from the project team

We've created this new test because of feedback about our usual platform called Maze.

This is an experiment and is the first time we have tested our designs in this way.

We will provide a link at the end of the test where you can submit your feedback on this format.
